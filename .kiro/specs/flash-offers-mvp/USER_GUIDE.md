## For Venue Owners: Creating Flash Offers

Flash Offers are time-limited promotions that you can send to nearby customers via push notifications. Here's how to create one:

### Step 1: Access the Flash Offers Dashboard

1. Open the app and navigate to your **Venue Dashboard**
2. Tap on the **"Flash Offers"** card
3. Tap the **"Create New Offer"** button

### Step 2: Fill Out the Offer Details

You'll need to provide the following information:

#### Basic Information
- **Title** (3-100 characters)
  - Keep it short and catchy
  - Example: "Happy Hour Special!" or "Free Appetizer"

- **Description** (10-500 characters)
  - Explain what customers will get
  - Include any terms or conditions
  - Example: "Get a free appetizer with any drink purchase. Valid until 7 PM today!"

- **Value Cap** (optional)
  - Maximum dollar value of the offer
  - Example: "$15" or "Up to $20"

#### Offer Limits
- **Maximum Claims** (1-1000)
  - How many customers can claim this offer
  - Once this limit is reached, the offer automatically closes
  - Example: 50 claims for a small promotion, 200 for a larger one

#### Timing
- **Start Time**
  - When the offer becomes active
  - Can be immediate or scheduled for later
  - Example: "Now" or "Today at 5:00 PM"

- **End Time**
  - When the offer expires
  - Must be after the start time
  - Example: "Today at 9:00 PM" or "In 2 hours"

#### Targeting
- **Radius** (default: 1 mile)
  - How far from your venue customers can see the offer
  - Options typically range from 0.5 to 5 miles

- **Target Favorites Only** (toggle)
  - If enabled, only customers who have favorited your venue will receive the push notification
  - If disabled, all nearby customers will be notified

- **Send Push Notification** (toggle)
  - If enabled, a push notification will be sent immediately when the offer goes live
  - Recommended for time-sensitive offers

### Step 3: Review and Submit

1. Review all the details carefully
2. Tap **"Create Offer"**
3. Wait for confirmation
4. Your offer is now live!

### Step 4: Monitor Your Offer

After creating an offer, you can:

- **View Real-Time Stats**
  - See how many people have viewed, claimed, and redeemed your offer
  - Track conversion rates

- **Manage Active Offers**
  - View all your offers grouped by status (Active, Scheduled, Expired)
  - Cancel an active offer if needed

- **Review Performance**
  - Check analytics after the offer ends
  - See metrics like open rate, claim rate, and redemption rate

### Tips for Successful Flash Offers

✅ **Do:**
- Create urgency with short time windows (1-3 hours)
- Use clear, compelling descriptions
- Set realistic claim limits based on your capacity
- Schedule offers during slower business hours
- Target your loyal customers (favorites) for better conversion

❌ **Don't:**
- Make offers too complicated or restrictive
- Set claim limits too low (customers may miss out)
- Schedule overlapping offers
- Forget to monitor redemptions

## For Customers: Claiming Flash Offers

Flash Offers are special time-limited deals from venues near you. Here's how to claim them:

### Step 1: Discover Flash Offers

You can find Flash Offers in several ways:

1. **Push Notifications**
   - You'll receive a notification when a nearby venue creates a new offer
   - Tap the notification to view details

2. **Home Screen**
   - Scroll to the "Flash Offers" section
   - See all active offers near your current location
   - Offers are sorted by distance and time remaining

3. **Venue Detail Page**
   - When viewing a venue, active offers will be displayed prominently

### Step 2: View Offer Details

Tap on any Flash Offer card to see:

- Full offer description
- Time remaining (live countdown)
- Claims remaining (e.g., "7 of 10 left")
- Venue information
- Terms and conditions

### Step 3: Check Eligibility

To claim a Flash Offer, you must:

✓ Be **checked in** to the venue
✓ The offer must still be **active** (not expired)
✓ Claims must still be **available** (not full)
✓ You haven't **already claimed** this offer

**Important:** You must be physically at the venue and checked in to claim an offer. This ensures the promotion is used by actual customers.

### Step 4: Claim the Offer

1. Make sure you're checked in to the venue
2. Tap the **"Claim Now"** button
3. Wait for confirmation (usually instant)
4. You'll see a success screen with your unique 6-digit token

### Step 5: Save Your Token

After claiming:

- Your **6-digit token** will be displayed prominently
- Take a screenshot or remember it
- The token expires after a set time (usually 24 hours)
- You can always find it later in "My Claims"

### Step 6: Redeem Your Offer

1. Show your 6-digit token to venue staff
2. Staff will enter the token in their system
3. Once verified, you'll receive your offer!
4. The token can only be used once

### Managing Your Claims

Access your claimed offers:

1. Go to your **Profile** or **Settings**
2. Tap **"My Flash Offers"**
3. View all your claims grouped by status:
   - **Active**: Ready to redeem
   - **Redeemed**: Already used
   - **Expired**: No longer valid

### Tips for Claiming Offers

✅ **Do:**
- Enable push notifications to get instant alerts
- Check in as soon as you arrive at a venue
- Claim offers quickly (they can fill up fast)
- Keep your token handy until redeemed
- Redeem before the expiration time

❌ **Don't:**
- Try to claim without checking in (it won't work)
- Share your token (it's unique to you)
- Wait too long to redeem (tokens expire)
- Attempt to claim the same offer twice

### Troubleshooting

**"You must be checked in to claim this offer"**
- Make sure you've checked in to the venue
- Check that your location services are enabled

**"This offer is no longer available"**
- The offer may have reached its claim limit
- Or it may have expired
- Try other available offers

**"You've already claimed this offer"**
- Each customer can only claim an offer once
- Check "My Claims" to find your token

## For Venue Staff: Redeeming Tokens

As venue staff, you'll help customers redeem their Flash Offer tokens. Here's how:

### Step 1: Access Token Redemption

1. Open the app and navigate to your **Venue Dashboard**
2. Tap on **"Redeem Token"** or **"Flash Offers"**
3. Select **"Redeem Token"** from the menu

### Step 2: Enter the Customer's Token

1. Ask the customer for their 6-digit token
2. Enter the token in the input field
   - The field accepts only numbers
   - Tokens are formatted with leading zeros (e.g., "004219")
3. Tap **"Validate Token"**

### Step 3: Verify the Claim

After validation, you'll see:

- Customer information
- Offer details (what they're claiming)
- When they claimed it
- Expiration status

**Check that:**
- The token belongs to your venue
- The claim hasn't been redeemed already
- The token hasn't expired
- The customer matches the claim (if needed)

### Step 4: Confirm Redemption

1. Review the claim details
2. Tap **"Confirm Redemption"**
3. Wait for confirmation
4. Provide the customer with their offer!

### Step 5: Success

- The claim is now marked as redeemed
- The customer cannot use this token again
- The redemption is recorded in your analytics

### Common Scenarios

#### Valid Token
```
Token: 004219
Status: ✓ Valid
Offer: Free Appetizer
Customer: John D.
Claimed: 2 hours ago
Action: Confirm Redemption
```

#### Already Redeemed
```
Token: 123456
Status: ✗ Already Redeemed
Redeemed by: Staff Member Name
Redeemed at: 3:45 PM
Action: Cannot redeem again
```

#### Expired Token
```
Token: 789012
Status: ✗ Expired
Offer: Happy Hour Special
Expired: 1 hour ago
Action: Cannot redeem
```

#### Invalid Token
```
Token: 999999
Status: ✗ Not Found
Action: Check the token and try again
```

### Tips for Staff

✅ **Do:**
- Double-check the token number before confirming
- Verify the customer is present
- Provide excellent service when redeeming offers
- Report any issues to management

❌ **Don't:**
- Redeem tokens without the customer present
- Share redemption access with unauthorized staff
- Override expired or invalid tokens
- Forget to confirm redemption in the system

### Troubleshooting

**"Token not found"**
- Ask the customer to verify the token number
- Check that they're at the correct venue
- Ensure they're showing the right offer

**"Token already redeemed"**
- This token has already been used
- Check with the customer if they've already received their offer
- They may be trying to use it twice

**"Token expired"**
- The claim has passed its expiration time
- Politely explain that the offer is no longer valid
- Suggest they check for other active offers

**Network errors**
- Ensure you have internet connectivity
- Try again in a moment
- If persistent, contact technical support

## Frequently Asked Questions

### For Venue Owners

**Q: How much does it cost to create a Flash Offer?**
A: Flash Offers are included with your venue account. You only pay for the value of the offers you provide to customers.

**Q: Can I cancel an active offer?**
A: Yes, you can cancel an active offer from the offer detail screen. However, customers who have already claimed will still be able to redeem their tokens.

**Q: What happens if my offer fills up quickly?**
A: The offer automatically closes when the claim limit is reached. No more customers can claim it, but existing claims can still be redeemed.

**Q: Can I create multiple offers at once?**
A: Yes, but we recommend focusing on one offer at a time for better results and easier management.

**Q: How do I know if my offer is performing well?**
A: Check the analytics dashboard to see open rates, claim rates, and redemption rates. A good offer typically has:
- Open rate: 30-50%
- Claim rate: 20-40% of views
- Redemption rate: 70-90% of claims

### For Customers

**Q: Do I need to be at the venue to claim an offer?**
A: Yes, you must be checked in to the venue to claim a Flash Offer. This ensures offers go to actual customers.

**Q: Can I claim multiple offers from the same venue?**
A: Yes, but only one of each specific offer. You can claim different offers from the same venue.

**Q: What if I forget my token?**
A: Go to "My Claims" in your profile to view all your active tokens.

**Q: Can I transfer my token to someone else?**
A: No, tokens are tied to your account and cannot be transferred.

**Q: What happens if I don't redeem my token in time?**
A: Expired tokens cannot be redeemed. Make sure to use your token before the expiration time.

### For Venue Staff

**Q: What if a customer's token shows as expired but they claim they just got it?**
A: Tokens have an expiration time set by the venue owner. Politely explain the policy and suggest they check for other active offers.

**Q: Can I redeem a token without the customer present?**
A: No, customers should be present when redeeming tokens to prevent fraud.

**Q: What if the system is down?**
A: Contact your manager or technical support. Do not manually honor offers without system confirmation.

**Q: How do I handle disputes?**
A: If a customer disputes a redemption issue, escalate to your manager with the token number and details.

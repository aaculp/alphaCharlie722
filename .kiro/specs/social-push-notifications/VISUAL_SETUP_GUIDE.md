### âœ… Recommended Settings

**iOS:**
- Allow Notifications: ON
- Lock Screen: ON
- Notification Center: ON
- Banners: ON
- Sounds: ON
- Badges: ON

**Android:**
- Show notifications: ON
- All categories: ON
- Sound: Enabled
- Vibration: Enabled

### ðŸ”‹ Battery Optimization

**iOS:**
- Background App Refresh: ON for OTW
- Low Power Mode: Notifications still work

**Android:**
- Battery optimization: Unrestricted or Optimized (not Restricted)
- Background data: Enabled for OTW

### ðŸ”• Do Not Disturb

Both iOS and Android respect Do Not Disturb mode:
- Notifications are delivered silently
- They appear when you disable Do Not Disturb
- You can allow OTW to bypass Do Not Disturb in device settings

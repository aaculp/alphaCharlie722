# Push Notifications Quick Start Guide

## Enable Push Notifications in 3 Steps

### Step 1: Allow Permissions
When you first open OTW, tap **"Allow"** when asked for notification permission.

### Step 2: Customize Your Preferences
1. Tap **Profile** tab
2. Tap **Settings** (gear icon)
3. Scroll to **Notifications**
4. Toggle notification types ON/OFF

### Step 3: You're Done!
You'll now receive instant notifications for social activities.

---

## Quick Troubleshooting

### Not receiving notifications?

**Quick Fix:**
1. Check device Settings > OTW > Notifications (ON)
2. Check OTW app Settings > Notifications (ON)
3. Restart the app

### Notifications delayed?

**Quick Fix:**
- Disable battery saver for OTW
- Check internet connection
- Enable Background App Refresh (iOS)

### Too many notifications?

**Quick Fix:**
1. Open OTW Settings
2. Go to Notifications
3. Disable specific types you don't want

## Review Guidelines

### What Makes a Great Review

#### Be Specific

Instead of: "It was good"
Try: "The espresso was perfectly balanced, and the barista was knowledgeable about their beans"

#### Be Honest

Share your genuine experience, both positive and negative aspects.

#### Be Fair

Consider the full context of your visit. One bad experience doesn't define a venue.

#### Be Helpful

Think about what information would help others decide whether to visit.

### What to Include

‚úÖ **Your Experience**
- What you ordered/did
- Service quality
- Atmosphere and ambiance
- Value for money
- Specific details that stood out

‚úÖ **Context**
- When you visited (time of day, day of week)
- How busy it was
- Any special circumstances

‚úÖ **Balanced Perspective**
- What you liked
- What could be improved
- Would you return?

### What to Avoid

‚ùå **Personal Attacks**
- Don't attack staff members personally
- Focus on service, not individuals
- Keep criticism constructive

‚ùå **Offensive Language**
- No profanity, hate speech, or slurs
- Keep it appropriate for all ages
- Be respectful in disagreements

‚ùå **Irrelevant Content**
- Stay on topic about the venue
- Don't use reviews for personal agendas
- No promotional content or spam

‚ùå **Private Information**
- Don't share phone numbers, addresses, or emails
- Don't reveal business secrets
- Respect others' privacy

‚ùå **Threats or Harassment**
- No threats of any kind
- No harassment or bullying
- No intimidation tactics

## Content Moderation

### Automatic Filtering

Our system automatically filters:
- **Mild Profanity**: Censored with asterisks (e.g., "f***")
- **Severe Content**: Rejected immediately (hate speech, threats)
- **Spam Patterns**: Blocked to prevent abuse

### What Gets Removed

Reviews may be removed if they contain:

1. **Hate Speech**: Attacks based on race, religion, gender, sexuality, disability, etc.
2. **Threats**: Any form of threat or intimidation
3. **Harassment**: Bullying, stalking, or targeted harassment
4. **Spam**: Promotional content, repetitive posts, or irrelevant content
5. **Fake Reviews**: Reviews from people who didn't visit the venue
6. **Personal Information**: Phone numbers, addresses, or other private data
7. **Illegal Content**: Anything promoting illegal activities
8. **Impersonation**: Pretending to be someone else

### Reporting Content

If you see content that violates these guidelines:

1. Tap the three-dot menu (‚ãÆ)
2. Select "Report"
3. Choose a reason
4. Provide details (optional)
5. Submit

Our moderation team reviews reports within 24 hours.

## Authenticity Standards

### Real Experiences Only

- ‚úÖ Review venues you've actually visited
- ‚úÖ Share your genuine experience
- ‚úÖ Be honest about your visit

- ‚ùå Don't review venues you haven't visited
- ‚ùå Don't write fake reviews
- ‚ùå Don't coordinate review campaigns

### Conflicts of Interest

**Disclose if you:**
- Work at the venue
- Own the venue
- Have a business relationship with the venue
- Received compensation or free items

**Example:**
> "Disclosure: I'm friends with the owner, but I'm sharing my honest experience as a customer."

### One Review Per Venue

- You can only submit one review per venue
- Edit your existing review to reflect new experiences
- Don't create multiple accounts to leave multiple reviews

## Venue Owner Guidelines

### Responding to Reviews

**Do:**
- ‚úÖ Respond professionally to all reviews
- ‚úÖ Thank customers for positive feedback
- ‚úÖ Apologize sincerely for negative experiences
- ‚úÖ Offer to make things right
- ‚úÖ Take criticism constructively

**Don't:**
- ‚ùå Argue with customers
- ‚ùå Be defensive or make excuses
- ‚ùå Threaten or intimidate reviewers
- ‚ùå Share customer private information
- ‚ùå Offer compensation for review changes

### Ethical Practices

**Allowed:**
- Asking customers to share their experience
- Displaying review prompts in your venue
- Thanking customers who leave reviews
- Responding to all reviews equally

**Not Allowed:**
- Offering incentives for positive reviews
- Asking only happy customers to review
- Writing fake reviews for your business
- Asking friends/family to write fake reviews
- Threatening legal action over honest reviews
- Paying for positive reviews
- Leaving negative reviews for competitors

## Privacy and Safety

### Protecting Your Privacy

- Don't share personal contact information
- Use your display name, not full legal name
- Don't include photos with identifiable people
- Be cautious about revealing your location
- Review privacy settings regularly

### Protecting Others' Privacy

- Don't share others' personal information
- Don't post photos of people without permission
- Don't reveal private business information
- Respect confidentiality

### Staying Safe

- Meet in public places if arranging meetups
- Don't share financial information
- Report suspicious behavior
- Trust your instincts

## Consequences for Violations

### Warning System

1. **First Violation**: Warning and content removal
2. **Second Violation**: Temporary suspension (7 days)
3. **Third Violation**: Permanent account suspension

### Immediate Suspension

Severe violations result in immediate permanent suspension:
- Hate speech or threats
- Harassment or bullying
- Coordinated fake review campaigns
- Impersonation
- Illegal activity

### Appeals Process

If you believe your content was removed in error:

1. Contact support@otw.com
2. Provide your username and review details
3. Explain why you believe it was removed incorrectly
4. Wait for review (typically 3-5 business days)

## Best Practices

2. **Be Timely**: Review soon after your visit
3. **Be Specific**: Include details that help others
4. **Be Fair**: Consider the full experience

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I delete a review I don't like?

**For Reviewers**: Yes, you can delete your own reviews anytime.

**For Venue Owners**: No, you cannot delete customer reviews. You can report reviews that violate guidelines, but honest negative reviews will remain.

### What if someone leaves a fake review?

Report it using the report function. Provide evidence if possible (e.g., the person never visited). Our team will investigate.

### Can I edit my review after posting?

Yes, you can edit your review anytime. An "Edited" indicator will show on updated reviews.

### What happens if I violate guidelines?

Depending on severity:
- Content removal
- Warning
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent ban

### How do I report a guideline violation?

Tap the three-dot menu (‚ãÆ) on the content and select "Report." Choose the appropriate reason and submit.

### Can venue owners pay to remove negative reviews?

No. We don't allow review removal for payment. Reviews can only be removed if they violate our guidelines.

### What if a venue owner threatens me over a review?

Report this immediately to support@otw.com. This is a serious violation and will result in the venue owner's account suspension.

### Can I review my own business?

No. Reviewing your own business is not allowed and will result in account suspension.

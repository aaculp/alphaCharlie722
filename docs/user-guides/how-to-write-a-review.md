# How to Write a Review

## Overview

Reviews help other users discover great venues and provide valuable feedback to venue owners. This guide explains how to write helpful, constructive reviews on OTW.

## Quick Start

1. Visit a venue
2. Check out when you leave
3. Rate your experience (1-5 stars)
4. Optionally add written feedback
5. Submit your review

## Writing Your First Review

### From the Check-Out Flow

The easiest way to leave a review is right after checking out:

1. **Check Out**: Tap the check-out button when leaving a venue
2. **Review Prompt**: A review prompt will appear automatically
3. **Select Vibe** (Optional): Choose the current atmosphere:

4. **Rate**: Tap 1-5 stars to rate your experience
5. **Add Details** (Optional): Tap "Add written review" for more feedback
6. **Submit**: Your rating is saved automatically!

### From the Venue Detail Screen

You can also write a review anytime from a venue's page:

1. **Open Venue**: Navigate to the venue detail screen
2. **Write Review**: Tap the "Write a Review" button
3. **Rate**: Select 1-5 stars (required)
4. **Write**: Add your thoughts (optional, up to 500 characters)
5. **Submit**: Tap "Submit Review"

When rating a venue, think about:

- **Service**: Was the staff friendly and helpful?
- **Atmosphere**: Did you enjoy the vibe and ambiance?
- **Quality**: Were the products/services high quality?
- **Value**: Was it worth the price?

## Writing Helpful Reviews

### Do's ‚úÖ

- **Be specific**: Mention what you liked or didn't like
- **Be honest**: Share your genuine experience
- **Be constructive**: Offer helpful feedback
- **Be fair**: Consider the full experience
- **Be timely**: Review soon after your visit

**Good Example:**
> "Great coffee and cozy atmosphere! The barista recommended the cold brew which was excellent. Only downside was limited seating during peak hours. Would definitely return for a quieter afternoon visit."

### Don'ts ‚ùå

- **Don't be vague**: "It was okay" doesn't help others
- **Don't be offensive**: Keep it respectful
- **Don't spam**: One review per venue
- **Don't be irrelevant**: Stay on topic
- **Don't share personal info**: Protect your privacy

**Bad Example:**
> "Terrible place. Worst ever. Never going back."

## Review Features

### Verified Reviews

Reviews from users who checked in to the venue show a "Verified Visit" badge. This helps others trust authentic experiences.

### Character Limit

Written reviews are limited to 500 characters. A character counter shows your remaining space as you type.

### Content Moderation

Our system automatically filters inappropriate language to maintain a respectful community. Severe violations (hate speech, threats) will be rejected.

## Helpful Votes

### Marking Reviews as Helpful

Found a review useful? Let others know:

1. Tap the "Helpful" button on any review
2. The count increases by 1
3. Tap again to remove your vote

**Note**: You can't vote on your own reviews.

### Why It Matters

Helpful votes highlight quality reviews for other users. Reviews with many helpful votes appear higher in the "Most Helpful" sort.

## Venue Owner Responses

Venue owners can respond to your review. You'll receive a notification when they do. This creates a dialogue and shows the venue cares about feedback.

## Community Guidelines

### Respectful Communication

- Treat others as you'd like to be treated
- Disagree respectfully
- Focus on the venue, not personal attacks
- Keep language appropriate for all ages

### Authentic Reviews

- Review only venues you've actually visited
- Don't review your own business
- Don't coordinate fake reviews
- Don't accept payment for reviews

### Privacy and Safety

- Don't share personal information (phone, address, etc.)
- Don't include photos of people without permission
- Don't reveal private business information
- Report suspicious activity

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I review a venue multiple times?

No, you can only submit one review per venue. However, you can edit your review anytime to reflect new experiences.

### Why can't I submit a review?

Common reasons:
- You've already reviewed this venue (edit your existing review instead)
- You've reached the rate limit (5 reviews per hour)
- Your review contains inappropriate content
- You're not signed in

### Will the venue owner see my review?

Yes, all reviews are public. Venue owners can see and respond to reviews.

### Can I make my review private?

No, all reviews are public to help other users make informed decisions. If you prefer not to share publicly, consider providing feedback directly to the venue.

### What happens to my reviews if I delete my account?

Your reviews will remain visible but will show as "Former User" instead of your name.

### How long does it take for my review to appear?

Reviews appear immediately after submission. There's no approval process unless content moderation flags your review.

### Can I add photos to my review?

Photo uploads are coming soon! For now, you can only submit star ratings and written text.

## Tips for Great Reviews

### Be Balanced

Mention both positives and areas for improvement. Even great venues have room to grow.

### Provide Context

Explain when you visited, what you ordered, and who you were with. Context helps others relate to your experience.

### Update When Things Change

If a venue improves (or declines), edit your review to reflect the current state.

### Respond to Owner Replies

If a venue owner responds to your review, consider replying to continue the conversation.

### Help Others Decide

Think about what information would help you choose a venue. Include those details in your review.
